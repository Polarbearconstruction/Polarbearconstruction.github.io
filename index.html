POLAR BEAR CONSTRUCTION

Polar Bear Construction specializes in foundation repair, waterproofing, and all exterior and interior work for residential and commercial structures. We ensure quality, reliability, and long-term durability.

Our Services

Foundation & Settlement Repair

Push piers, helical piers, slab piers, and crawlspace support systems to stabilize and lift structures.

Wall Repair Systems

Carbon fiber reinforcement, wall stitching, and IntelliBrace systems to fix cracks and bowing walls.

Waterproofing

Basement drainage systems, wall sealing, sump connections, and moisture control solutions.

Excavation

Hand digging and machine excavation for shallow to deep foundation access.

Interior Structural Work

Joist reinforcement, drywall removal, insulation removal, and support beam installation.

Concrete Repair

Crack repair, joint sealing, demolition, and replacement of damaged concrete surfaces.
