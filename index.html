Our Services

Settlement Repair

  • Push Pier: Steel piers driven deep into stable soil to lift and stabilize sinking foundations.
  • Helical Pier: Screw-like piers installed into the ground to support and stabilize structures.
  • Helical Pier (Handheld): Used in tight or limited-access areas where machinery cannot fit.
  • Crawlspace Pier: Provides support to sagging crawlspaces and uneven floors.
  • Concrete Remove & Replace: Damaged concrete is removed and replaced with new, reinforced material.
  • Custom Concrete Demo: Safe demolition of existing concrete structures.
  • Slab Pier (with Poly Fill): Lifts and stabilizes concrete slabs using polyurethane injection.

Engineering

  • Engineering Assessment: Professional inspection and structural analysis when required.
  • Utilities Protection: Ensures all underground utilities are protected during work.

Wall Repair

  • Carbon Fiber Strip: Reinforces cracked or bowing walls with high-strength carbon fiber.
  • Carbon Fiber Stitching: Stabilizes cracks using stitching techniques for long-term durability.
  • IntelliBrace System: Adjustable steel braces used to straighten and stabilize walls.

Wall Excavation

  • Hand Dig (0'–4'): Manual excavation for small or tight areas.
  • Machine Access (0'–4'): Machine excavation for shallow work.
  • Machine Access (5'–9'): Mid-depth excavation for foundation repairs.
  • Machine Access (9'+): Deep excavation for major structural work.

IntelliJacks

  • 1'–3': Supports minor floor sagging.
  • 3'–5': Reinforces moderate structural issues.
  • 5'–8': Stabilizes significant floor and beam problems.
  • 8'+: Heavy-duty support for major structural concerns.
  • Cast-in-Place Footing: Concrete base for long-term jack stability.
  • Supplemental Beam: Additional beam support for load distribution.

Interior Structural Work

  • Joist Reinforcement: Strengthens weakened wooden floor joists.
  • Drywall Removal (Full/Half Wall): Safe removal for access or renovation.
  • Insulation Removal: Removal of old or damaged insulation materials.

Waterproofing

  • BasementGutter: Interior drainage system to redirect water away.
  • WallSeal: Waterproof barrier applied to foundation walls.
  • Lateral Line: Redirects water flow away from foundation.
  • Feed Line: Connects drainage to sump system.
  • Basement Air System: Controls moisture and improves air quality.

Concrete Repair

  • Control Joint Caulking: Seals joints to prevent cracking and water entry.
  • Crack Repair: Repairs and seals concrete cracks for durability.

Site Work & Miscellaneous

  • Site Work / Obstructions: Clearing and preparation for construction work.
  • Mobilization: Travel and setup for jobs over long distances.
  • Depth Clause: Adjustments for deeper-than-expected work.
  • Dumpster Fee: Waste removal and disposal services.
